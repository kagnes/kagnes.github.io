I am a computational corpus linguist at the Hungarian Research Centre for Linguistics in Budapest. I specialize in data-driven research of Hungarian preverb constructions (see {{< staticref "project/phd" >}}my Ph.D. dissertation{{< /staticref >}}). I am interested in Natural Language Processing, especially in fine-tuning existing tools and developing new ones for corpus building and dictionary editing. I was among the creators of the {{< staticref "http://oldhungariancorpus.nytud.hu/en-descr.html" "newtab" >}}Old Hungarian Corpus{{< /staticref >}}, the {{< staticref "http://parallelbible.nytud.hu/" "newtab" >}}Parallel Bible Reader{{< /staticref >}} and the {{< staticref "http://www.nytud.hu/depts/tlp/uralic/dbases.html" "newtab" >}}UraLUID{{< /staticref >}} database. Apart from my academic work, I am a freelance language technologist and a language enthusiast.
